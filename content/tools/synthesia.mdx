## What is Synthesia? (And Why I Switched to It)

I discovered Synthesia when I was drowning in video requests from clients. As a consultant, I was spending entire weekends recording training videos, doing multiple takes, and wrestling with editing software just to create simple explainer content.

Synthesia is an AI video platform that generates professional videos using realistic avatars — no cameras, microphones, or editing required. You paste in your script, pick an avatar, and get a polished video in minutes. Unlike HeyGen which focuses on avatar cloning, Synthesia prioritizes ease of use and multilingual content creation.

## What I Actually Use It For

My main use case is creating onboarding videos for SaaS clients. Last month, I produced 12 product walkthrough videos for a project management tool launch. Each video took 15 minutes to create in Synthesia versus the 3+ hours I used to spend recording and editing.

I also use it for course content. When I launched a digital marketing course, I needed 25 lesson videos quickly. Synthesia let me batch-create all of them in two afternoons. The consistent avatar appearance across videos gives the course a professional, unified look.

The multilingual feature is a game-changer. I created the same product demo in English, Spanish, and French by simply translating the script — same avatar, same timing, perfect lip-sync in all three languages.

## The Features That Matter

### Avatar Selection and Quality
Synthesia offers 140+ avatars across different ethnicities, ages, and presentation styles. I primarily use "Sarah" for professional content and "Marcus" for technical demos. The lip-sync accuracy impressed me — it's nearly indistinguishable from real speech, unlike the robotic movement I see in cheaper tools like Pictory.

### Script-to-Video Generation
You paste your script (up to 10 minutes of content per video), select an avatar and background, then wait 5-8 minutes for processing. I tested this with a 1,200-word script and got a polished 8-minute video. The AI handles pacing and pauses naturally.

### Custom Branding Options
The Corporate plan lets you upload logos, use custom colors, and add intro/outro sequences. I white-label videos for clients regularly — the branding customization makes them look like they were produced in-house with a professional video team.

### What's Missing
I wish they offered more gesture variety — avatars mostly stay static from the chest up. Voice cloning is locked behind the expensive Enterprise tier, which is frustrating since competitors like Murf include it at lower price points.

|------|-------|-------------|-----------|
| Personal | $29/mo | 10 videos/month, 120+ avatars, 65+ languages | Good for solopreneurs and small content needs |
| Corporate | $90/mo | 30 videos/month, custom avatars, team collaboration | Best value for agencies and growing businesses |
| Enterprise | Custom | Unlimited videos, voice cloning, API access | Only worth it for large organizations |

The Corporate plan offers the best value if you're creating more than 10 videos monthly. At $90/month, you're paying $3 per video versus $2.90 on Personal — but you get custom branding and team features that justify the upgrade.

Unlike Loom's simple screen recording or Camtasia's complex editing, Synthesia sits perfectly in the middle for professional avatar-based content.

## Who Should (and Shouldn't) Use Synthesia

**Good fit if you:**
- Create training videos, product demos, or educational content regularly
- Need multilingual versions of the same content
- Want professional-looking videos without learning video editing

**Skip it if you:**
- Need highly animated or creative video content
- Require extensive customization beyond basic branding
- Create less than 5 videos per month (not cost-effective)

After four months of regular use, Synthesia has become essential to my content workflow. The combination of speed, quality, and multilingual capabilities makes it the best AI avatar video maker I've tested — superior to HeyGen's clunky interface and Pictory's limited avatar selection.
