## The Features That Matter

This is Writesonic's crown jewel and why it's the best affordable AI writer for blogs. You input a keyword, choose article length (500-3,000 words), and select your tone. In my testing, it consistently produces structured articles with proper H2/H3 headings, intro paragraphs, and conclusions. ### ChatSonic Integration  
Unlike Jasper which keeps chat and long-form writing separate, Writesonic integrates both seamlessly. ChatSonic has access to current Google data (not just training data from 2021), so when I write about recent industry trends, the information stays current. I've fact-checked dozens of claims, and the accuracy rate sits around 90%.

### Bulk Generation Tools
The CSV import feature lets me upload 50+ blog topics at once and generate drafts in batches. Each generation takes 30-45 seconds, so I can produce a month's worth of content in under an hour. Jasper charges extra for bulk features, but Writesonic includes it in the $20 Pro plan.

Writesonic lacks a built-in plagiarism checker, which means I run everything through Copyscape separately. Also, the image generation feature produces mediocre results compared to dedicated tools like Midjourney — I wish they'd integrate with better image AI models.
