## What is Jasper AI?

Jasper AI (formerly Jarvis) is an enterprise-grade AI writing platform built for marketing teams. It uses advanced language models to generate blog posts, social media content, ad copy, and more — all while maintaining your brand voice.

## Key Features

### Brand Voice
Upload your style guide and Jasper learns to write in your tone. This is a game-changer for teams managing multiple brands.

### Templates
Over 50 templates cover everything from blog outlines to Google Ads headlines. Each template is optimized for its specific use case.

### Long-Form Editor
The Boss Mode editor handles full blog posts, whitepapers, and ebooks. It maintains context across thousands of words.

## Who Should Use Jasper?

Jasper is ideal for marketing teams, content agencies, and businesses that produce content at scale. If you need brand consistency across a team, Jasper delivers.

## The Bottom Line

Jasper AI earns its reputation as the leading AI writing tool for teams. While the price tag is steep for individuals, the ROI for businesses is clear.
