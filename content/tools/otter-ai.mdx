## What is Otter.ai? (And Why I Switched to It)

I was drowning in client meeting notes. Six months ago, I was manually transcribing 8-10 sales calls weekly, taking 4x the meeting length to get usable notes. Otter.ai is an AI-powered transcription service that joins your video calls automatically and generates meeting summaries in real-time.

Unlike Fireflies.ai which focuses heavily on CRM integration, Otter prioritizes accuracy and speed. After testing both for 3 months on identical client calls, Otter consistently delivered cleaner transcripts with fewer hallucinations.

## What I Actually Use It For

My primary use case is transcribing discovery calls with potential clients. I have Otter join every Zoom meeting automatically, and within 30 seconds of ending the call, I have a searchable transcript with action items highlighted. This replaced my old workflow of recording locally and uploading to Rev, which cost $1.50 per audio hour and took 12+ hours for turnaround.

I also use it for internal team standups. Our 15-minute daily check-ins generate 2,500-3,000 word transcripts that I can search later when someone asks "What did Sarah say about the Johnson project last Tuesday?" The search function finds exact quotes in under 3 seconds.

The most valuable feature is the summary generation. After a 45-minute strategy session, Otter produces a 200-word summary with 4-6 bullet points of key decisions. I copy-paste this directly into project management tools, saving 15 minutes of manual note-taking per meeting.

## The Features That Matter

### Live Transcription with Speaker ID
The real-time transcription appears with a 2-3 second delay, accurate enough that I stopped taking manual notes entirely. Speaker identification works flawlessly with up to 6 people in my experience, though it claims to handle 10. I tested this with a 8-person team meeting and it correctly labeled speakers 85% of the time.

### AI-Generated Summaries
After each meeting, Otter creates a summary with action items, key topics, and decisions made. These summaries average 150-250 words for 30-60 minute meetings. The quality is solid — I'd say 80% usable without editing, compared to Grain.com's summaries which feel too generic.

### Calendar Integration and Auto-Join
Otter connects to Google Calendar and Outlook, automatically joining scheduled Zoom or Teams meetings. This eliminated the friction of remembering to start recording. Unlike Sembly.ai which requires manual setup for each call, Otter just works in the background.

### What's Missing
Custom vocabulary training is locked behind the $30/month Business plan, which frustrated me when dealing with industry-specific terms. Also missing: the ability to create custom summary templates like Fireflies offers.

The Pro plan gives you 100 hours monthly, which covers my 20-25 weekly meetings comfortably. At $16.99, it's cheaper than Rev's $22/month plan and infinitely faster. Fireflies charges $18/month for similar features, but their accuracy isn't as consistent in my testing.

## Who Should (and Shouldn't) Use Otter.ai

**Good fit if you:**
- Attend 5+ meetings weekly that need documentation
- Work with clear English speakers (minimal heavy accents)
- Use Zoom, Teams, or Google Meet as primary meeting platforms
- Need transcripts within minutes, not hours

**Skip it if you:**
- Primarily record phone calls (mobile app is buggy for long recordings)
- Need perfect accuracy for legal/medical use (still requires human review)
- Work mainly with non-English speakers (accuracy drops to 70-75%)

## Final Verdict

After 6 months of daily use across 200+ client calls, Otter.ai has become indispensable to my workflow. The combination of real-time accuracy and automatic meeting joining eliminated the friction that made me avoid transcription tools in the past.

I'd recommend the Pro plan at $16.99/month for anyone conducting 5+ meetings weekly. The time savings alone — roughly 2 hours per week for me — justifies the cost. If you're still manually taking meeting notes or waiting hours for Rev transcripts, try Otter's free plan with your next 5 meetings.
